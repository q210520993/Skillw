      • ignite

         abstract void ignite()

        点燃此苦力怕, 开始其爆炸计时. 苦力怕从点燃到爆炸所需时间取决于setMaxFuseTicks的设置,默认值为30. 使用本方法引起的爆炸可被org.bukkit.event.entity.ExplosionPrimeEvent取消, 并且遵循其所处世界的mobGriefing游戏规则.

        原文:Ignites this Creeper, beginning its fuse. The amount of time the Creeper takes to explode will depend on what setMaxFuseTicks is set as. The resulting explosion can be cancelled by an org.bukkit.event.entity.ExplosionPrimeEvent and obeys the mob griefing gamerule.
