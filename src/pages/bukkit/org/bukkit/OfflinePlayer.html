Interface OfflinePlayer

  • All Implemented Interfaces:
    org.bukkit.configuration.serialization.ConfigurationSerializable , org.bukkit.entity.AnimalTamer , org.bukkit.permissions.ServerOperator 
    
public interface OfflinePlayer
 implements ServerOperator, AnimalTamer, ConfigurationSerializable

    代表一个对玩家身份及其附属数据的引用, 数据存储于磁盘中, 因此不需要玩家在线即可获取到玩家的引用.
